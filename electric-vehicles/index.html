Warning Signs

EV issues can escalate quickly — don't ignore these signals.

Reduced driving range or sudden range drop
Charging not completing or taking much longer
Battery warning or EV system fault light
Unusual sounds from motor or drivetrain
Regenerative braking feeling inconsistent
12V battery draining frequently
