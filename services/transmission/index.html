Warning Signs

Transmission problems don't fix themselves — act early.

Slipping gears or delayed engagement
Burning smell from under the vehicle
Grinding or shaking during gear changes
Transmission warning light on dashboard
Leaking red or brown transmission fluid
Rough or jerky acceleration
