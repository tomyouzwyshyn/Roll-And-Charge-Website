Warning Signs

Don't ignore these — suspension issues worsen quickly.

Bouncing or excessive body roll on turns
Vehicle nose-dives when braking
Uneven or rapid tire wear
Steering feels loose or wandering
Knocking or clunking over bumps
Vehicle sits lower on one side
