Warning Signs

Engine issues rarely resolve on their own — get checked early.

Check engine light is on or flashing
Loss of power or sluggish acceleration
Knocking or ticking sounds from engine
Excessive oil or coolant consumption
White, blue, or black exhaust smoke
Engine overheating or temperature spikes
