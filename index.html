Frequently Asked

Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about Roll & Charge mobile and in-shop service.

Do you really come to my location for tire installation and auto service?

Yes. Our mobile service vans handle tire installation, brake work, diagnostics, batteries, and most maintenance at your home, office, or roadside — typically same-day. Larger jobs route to our shop.

How much does mobile tire installation cost compared to a traditional shop?

Our mobile installation is priced transparently and is typically comparable to or below national chain shops once you factor in fuel, time off work, and waiting-room hours saved. You see the full price before booking — no surprise fees.

Do you service electric and hybrid vehicles?

Yes. Our certified EV technicians handle Tesla, Rivian, Ford Lightning, Lucid, and most hybrid and electric models — including high-voltage battery diagnostics, charging-system repair, tire and brake work, and our proprietary on-site EV charging top-up for roadside calls.

Are your technicians certified?

All Roll & Charge technicians are ASE-certified with manufacturer-specific training for both ICE and EV platforms. Our work is backed by a written warranty on parts and labor.

Can I book same-day service?

Most appointments — including tire installation, brake service, batteries, and diagnostics — are available same-day. Book in the morning, drive on new tires by afternoon.

Do you handle fleet vehicles?

Yes. We service commercial fleets with on-site preventive maintenance, scheduled tire rotation, brake service, and DOT inspections at your yard or job site — keeping your vehicles in service instead of off the road.
